Gobble Systems Support Policy

 

 

 

 

This is the systems support policy (Support Policy) of KBNT Pty Ltd (ACN 636 191 243) (Gobble). Gobble is subject to and is governed by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). This policy is to be read in conjunction with the Gobble’s Terms and Conditions dated April 2020 and Privacy Policy dated 2020.

References

Any reference to support in this document is a reference to Technical Support provided by Swayy Application Designers (Swayy).

Any reference to the App is a reference to the Gobble App downloaded form the Apple App Store or the Android Play Store. Any reference to Gobble Platform is a reference to the App and to the mygobble.com website.

Any reference to the System is a reference to the Gobble App which participants register their details with to allow them to use the App.

This Support Policy applies to all participants who register to use the App and System. This includes:-

  • Restaurants registered as vendors on the System (Merchants)
  • Users registered to use the App (Customers); and
  • Employees of the Vendors that will be conducting deliveries (Drivers and Garçon). Any reference to a Garcon on the App is a reference to a Driver.

This Support Policy outlines the technical support that Gobble will provide to all registered users of the App and the Gobble Platform as a whole.

 

Technical Support

To use Gobble you must be operating a smart phone. Gobble will not be functional on any other platform and cannot commit to operating smoothly on technology that does not allow for the download of third party applications through the Apple App Store or the Android Play Store.

Gobble in conjunction with Swayy are committed to ensuring all registered Merchants, Customers and Drivers are provided around the clock support where there is a technical fault with the App. The operators of Gobble remain committed to ensuring that the App operates smoothly.

If any user of the App experiences a technical problem while on the App you should contact the below email address with details of the issue they are experiencing:

  • Email Address: info@sway.com.au

Once Swayy receives your correspondence, they will investigate the problem and attend to correcting any technical errors occurring.

Gobble and Swayy consider all communications they receive about the functionality of the App as a matter of priority and are therefore dedicated to ensuring all communications received will be responded to.

If your issue requires an urgent response please mark your email as URGENT. Swayy will prioritise enquiries marked with this.

Any email sent through to the info line referred to prior will be responded to within 24 hours of Swayy receiving the communication.

 

Ongoing Maintenance

To ensure smooth operation of Gobble at all times, Swayy will attend to ongoing maintenance of the App at their own discretion. Registered users may not be notified prior to all updates being completed.

 

Financial Support

Gobble uses the platform Stripe for all payments through the App. Stripe is an industry recognized finance platform that provides the economic infrastructure Gobble relies on to process all financials involved with the App. Stripe are committed to work with Gobble registered users to ensures that no Customer, Merchant or Driver experiences a problem with making  payment or receiving one.

Stripe uses only the latest technology to ensure the security of Gobble’s online economy is upheld. As a leader  in the E-Commerce world, Stripe are committed to Gobble’s cause and are available 24 x 7 to be contacted for any finance related issues on the App.

If any Customers, Merchants or Drivers experience a problem in relation to receiving a payment or making a payment they must direct their enquiry to Stripe directly. Contact details can be found on their website: www.stripe.com

 

Issues with orders  

If any Customer wishes to raise a complaint or a praise of any Merchant they are to contact the restaurant directly. Gobble holds no liability for the delivery of food or the food itself.

If a Customer requires a refund or has a problem with the quality of their order, they are to contact the Merchant directly and not through the App. Gobble holds no responsibility as to the quality of the food, errors or omissions by the Merchant and the Drivers in the preparation and delivery of the food and beverage ordered by a user.

 

Functionality

Align with Gobble’s Terms and Conditions for Merchants and Customers, once a Driver has been allocated to a Customer’s order by a Merchant, the Merchant and the Customer will both be able to contact the Driver directly. Once the delivery is complete, the ability to contact the Driver will cease. Customers are permitted ONLY to contact the Driver to provide delivery related instructions.

 

Complaints and Compliments Management

Merchant, Customers and Drivers

If a party wishes to make any communication with a particular Merchant, Driver they are to contact the Merchant directly to relay their communication.

If Gobble or any of their functioning platforms are flagged that any registered user is exhibiting anti social, abusive or harassing behaviour, they will immediately be de-registered from the App. 

Gobble will not tolerate any anti social, abusive or harassing behaviour towards any Vendor, user or Driver. Immediate cessation of registration will occur to a registered party engaging in such behaviour at the discretion of Gobble’s Operators.

No refunds of any kind will be issued to any Merchant, Customer or Driver if they are de-registered on grounds of anti social, abusive or harassing behaviours.

By signing up to use Gobble you agree to act within the guidelines of Gobble’s Customer Terms and Conditions and Gobble’s Merchant Terms and Conditions found on the Gobble Platforms.

If for any reason you do need to contact Gobble directly for support issues related to a Gobble Platform, enquiries should be sent to: mygobble1@gmail.com .

Changes to this Policy

Gobble reserves the right to modify this Policy from time to time, in its sole discretion, and the modified Policy shall be effective immediately after you have been notified that the policy has modified.
